Theme: Towards Innovation and Excellence in Medical and Pharmaceutical Research

Welcome to the Second International Medical and Pharmaceutical Sciences Conference (IUPMC 2025), hosted by the Islamic University in Najaf. Building upon the success of the first conference, this international event aims to bring together researchers, academics, healthcare professionals, and industry experts from around the world to discuss recent advances, exchange knowledge, and establish collaborations that shape the future of medical and pharmaceutical sciences.

About the Conference

The conference serves as an international platform to:

  • Present cutting-edge research in pharmaceutical and medical sciences.
  • Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration between academia, healthcare, and industry.
  • Provide opportunities for young researchers to showcase their work.
  • Facilitate partnerships with international journals and publishers for high-impact publication.

Conference Tracks & Topics

The scientific program will cover the following tracks:

1. Pharmacognosy

Natural products, herbal medicines, pharmacological evaluations.

2. Drug Delivery Systems

Nanotechnology, biotechnology, advanced delivery mechanisms.

3. Clinical Trials

Drug safety, efficacy testing, regulatory aspects.

4. Medicinal Chemistry

Drug design, synthesis, molecular modeling, chemical technologies.

5. Dentistry

Restorative, cosmetic, and preventive dentistry; oral health innovations.

6. Medical Sciences

Clinical medicine, diagnostics, health technology, and biomedical engineering.

Submission & Publication

  • Submissions are managed via the Open Journal System (OJS).
  • All papers undergo plagiarism detection using Turnitin.
  • Peer review is conducted by the Scientific Committee.
  • Accepted papers will be published in:
    • Iraqi Journal of Pharmaceutical Sciences
    • Indian Journal of Heterocyclic Chemistry
    • PLOS ONE
